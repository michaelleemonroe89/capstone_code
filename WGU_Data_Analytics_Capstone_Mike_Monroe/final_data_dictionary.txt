final.csv Data Dictionary
========================

StateName: Two-letter state abbreviation (e.g., AK, AL, CA).
year: Year of observation (integer, e.g., 2008).
median_sale_price: Median sale price of homes for that state and year (float, USD).
mortgage_rate: Median 30-year fixed mortgage interest rate for that year (float, percent).
monthly_payment: Estimated monthly mortgage payment for the median sale price and mortgage rate, assuming a 30-year loan (float, USD).
income: Median household income for that state and year (integer, USD).
pct_income_monthly_payment: Percentage of annual income required to cover 12 months of the estimated monthly payment (float, percent).
